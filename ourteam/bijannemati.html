Bijan Nemati

Bijan Nemati is a senior scientist and President of Tellus1.

He received his doctorate in high energy physics from the University of Washington, and worked at Lockheed Martin, the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, and the University of Alabama prior to founding Tellus1.
